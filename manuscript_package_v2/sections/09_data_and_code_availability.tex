\section*{Data and Code Availability}

City1 v2 is presented as a frozen \texttt{v2} manuscript package. The figures, tables, and narrative claims in the main paper are tied to the same frozen evidence stack and are generated from the reproducible \texttt{paper\_v2\_baseline} workflow. Official city totals are compiled within the frozen workflow from referenced official statistical sources, and the main paper does not introduce results beyond those contained in the frozen package. Expanded qualitative cases, the validation-evidence matrix, and technical appendix material on the city registry, feature inventory, weak-target weights, and calibration fallback behavior are provided in the supplement rather than in the main manuscript. The manuscript therefore reflects a fixed analytical baseline rather than an evolving experimental system.
